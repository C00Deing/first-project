How to win

the game is won by having the most points by the end of the three rounds

How to score points

You score points by getting rid of your cards quickly if you get rid of them first you get 30pts second 20pts third 10pts and you get nothing if you are last!

Note that if you got first place in the last round and you can't get rid of your cards first you automaticly get 0pts for the round

Trading cards

this happens at the start of round 2 and 3

Beggar(4th place) (must give away two of their highest-ranked cards) ↔ Tycoon(1st place) (must give away any two cards) Poor(3rd place)(must give away their highest-ranked card) ↔ Rich(2nd place)(must give away any one card)

How to get rid of cards

you can play cards in a single, pair, triple, and quaduple

you must play to beat the card currently on the table a single can only be beaten by a single a pair by a pair and so on

Card rankings and rules

The cards rank standerd from 3-ace but 2 outranks ace and is the highest ranking

jokers can serve as wildcards and gan be substituted for any card in a set

if a joker is played as a single it is considerd the highest card

Revolution and Counterrevolution

At the start of a trick If 4 cards of the same type(4 7s) is played all values are inverted

this makes 2 the lowist and 3 the highest

If a higher set of 4 cards is played during the turn this causes a Counterrevolution and the rankings retern to normal
