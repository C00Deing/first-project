How to win

the game is won by having the most points by the end of the three rounds

How to score points

You score points by getting rid of your cards quickly if you get rid of them first you get 30pts second 20pts third 10pts and you get nothing if you are last!

Note that if you got first place in the last round and you can't get rid of your cards first you automaticly get 0pts for the round

Trading cards

this happens at the start of round 2 and 3
